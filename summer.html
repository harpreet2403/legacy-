For more than five decades, Lilly has been dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that accelerate the pace and progress of cancer care and enhance the lives of people living with cancer.

This website is for the public and contains generic information about Lung Cancer and Gastric Cancer. There are also some helpful links listed below that can provide information on each of these diseases.

Please also remember to discuss any questions or concerns you have with your doctor or healthcare professional.

For more information, please visit the websites listed below.

 

Roy Castle Lung Cancer Foundation – Understanding Lung Cancer

Information to help you understand lung cancer, available treatment options and practical information on living with a lung cancer diagnosis.



http://www.roycastle.org/lung-cancer

United Kingdom Lung Cancer Coalition - UKLCC

The UK Lung Cancer Coalition (UKLCC) is a coalition of the UK’s leading lung cancer experts, senior NHS professionals, charities and healthcare companies. The UKLCC’s key objectives are to raise political and public awareness of lung cancer to improve services and patient outcomes in the UK.


http://www.uklcc.org.uk

Mesothelioma UK – Information and support on Mesothelioma

Mesothelioma UK is a national resource centre dedicated to providing specialist Mesothelioma information, support, improving care and treatment.



http://www.mesothelioma.uk.com/information-and-support/

Cancer Research UK

Patient information website of Cancer Research UK.



http://www.cancerresearchuk.org/about-cancer/

Macmillan Cancer Support

Macmillan Cancer Support provide high quality, up-to-date cancer information for cancer patients, their families and carers. Macmillian Cancer support state that all of their information is written and reviewed by specialists.


http://www.macmillan.org.uk/Cancerinformation/Cancerinformation.aspx
